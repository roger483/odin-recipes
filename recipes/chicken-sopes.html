Chicken Sopes Recipe

What Are Chicken Sopes?

Chicken sopes, or "sopes de pollo" in Spanish, are a type of dish that consist of a round, fried puck of maiz similar to a tortilla but thicker and smaller in diameter than a tortilla, that is usually topped with a protein and a sauce. Common toppings are chicken, beef, and refried beans, which are then covered with a guacamole sauce or sour cream (or both! yum!) and finished with lettuce, radish slices, or onions. The best thing about sopes is that you get to choose the various toppings you want and there's no right way to make them. If served as an appetizer, one or two will suffice but if they are the main dish, four would complete a full dish.

Ingredients:

  • Tomato salsa
  • Guacamole sauce
  • Chicken
  • Beans
  • Cheese
  • Sour cream

Optional:

  • Sliced radishes
  • Sliced onions
