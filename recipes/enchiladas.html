Enchiladas

What are enchiladas?

Enchiladas are a simple but delicious Mexican dish consisting of tortillas stuffed with ingredients like chicken and cheese, rolled up, dipped in a sauce, and then deep fried. They are then topped with sour cream and cheese. If you have a sensitive stomach, then they may be chorro-inducing.

Ingredients:

  • Yellow corn tortillas
  • dried chile de arbol
  • garlic
  • onion
  • chihuahua cheese
  • sour cream
  • queso fresco

Steps:

  1. Make salsa roja
  2. Put cheese (and optionally: chicken) on tortilla
  3. Roll it up
  4. Dip rolled up tortilla into red salsa, then throw into oiled pan, or the oven at 400 degrees for 20 minutes

    5. You don't really want to fry until hard, just until the tortilla is cooked and the cheese is fully melted.
